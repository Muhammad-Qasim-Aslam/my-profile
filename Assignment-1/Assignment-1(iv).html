Computer VS. Laptop

Computer:

A computer is an electronic device that processes, stores, and retrieves data. It consists of hardware components like the CPU, memory, storage, and input/output devices. Computers are used for various tasks and come in different forms, such as personal computers, laptops, servers, and mobile devices.

Laptop:

A laptop is a portable personal computer that combines a keyboard, display screen, and trackpad into a single compact unit. It is designed for mobility, runs on battery power, and allows users to perform various tasks and applications on the go.

Desi Cuisine

  • Biryani

    Biryani is a fragrant rice dish originating from the Indian subcontinent, prepared with basmati rice, meat, vegetables, and a blend of aromatic spices. The ingredients are layered and cooked together to create a flavorful and aromatic dish.
    Only In 220/- Rs.

  • Karahi

    Karahi is a flavorful South Asian dish cooked in a deep pan called a karahi. It features tender meat or vegetables cooked with spices like ginger, garlic, and a blend of aromatic spices. The dish is known for its rich flavors, and vibrant colors.
    Only In 750/- Rs. (Full)

  • Nihari

    Nihari is a slow-cooked meat stew known for its rich flavors. Tender meat, often beef or lamb, is simmered with spices for hours, creating a flavorful gravy. Nihari is garnished with ginger, green chilies, and lemon for added tanginess, making it a beloved and hearty dish.
    Only In 220/- Rs. (Single)

  • Haleem

    Haleem is a thick and flavorful stew made by slow-cooking a mixture of lentils, meat, and wheat grains. A blend of spices adds to its rich taste. Garnished with fried onions and fresh herbs, and is cherished for its comforting and satisfying qualities.
    Only In 200/- Rs. (Single)

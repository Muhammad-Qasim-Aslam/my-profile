Bar Be Que

  • Seekh Kabab

    A wrap of spiced chicken with vegetables inside a soft golden paratha, sometimes coated with eggs or drizzled with green chutney. Small cubes or slices of cooked chicken are stir fried with onion, capsicum and other spices.
    Only In 150/- Rs.

  • Chicken Boti

    Seekh boti is dish made from succulent pieces of marinated meat, typically lamb or beef, threaded onto skewers and cooked over open flames or charcoal. The tender and flavorful meat, infused with aromatic spices like cumin, coriander, and chili, makes seekh boti a delicious and satisfying culinary delight.
    Only In 175/- Rs.

  • Chicken Tikka

    Chicken tikka is a dish consisting of chicken pieces marinated in a blend of yogurt and spices like turmeric, cumin, and garam masala. The marinated chicken is then grilled or baked to perfection, resulting in tender, juicy, and flavorful morsels.
    Only In 250/- Rs.

  • Chargha

    Chargha is dish known for its whole roasted chicken. The chicken is marinated in a mixture of spices, including ginger, garlic, cumin, and red chili powder. It is then deep-fried or baked until it becomes crispy and golden brown on the outside while remaining juicy and flavorful on the inside.
    Only In 950/- Rs.
